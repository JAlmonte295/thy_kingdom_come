How to Play

Your Goal: Prepare your kingdom to withstand an enemy invasion at the end of the 7th day. Your leadership will determine if your kingdom stands victorious or crumbles to dust.

Daily Actions:

Each day is divided into three phases: Morning, Afternoon, and Evening. In each phase, you can perform one action. Choose wisely, as your resources are limited.

  • Military, Structure, Food, Culture: Spend 5 Treasury to increase the corresponding stat by 10.
  • Taxes: Gain 10 Treasury, but at the cost of 5 Popularity.

Managing Your Kingdom:

  • Treasury (💰): Your primary resource for all actions.
  • Popularity (😊): Keep your people happy! If your Popularity drops to 0, your people will revolt and you will lose the game.
  • Food (🍎) & Culture (🎨): These are vital for your people's morale. At the end of each day:
    • If both Food and Culture are high (30+), your Popularity will increase.
    • If either Food or Culture is too low (below 20), your Popularity will decrease.

Winning the Game:

On the final day, your kingdom's strength will be tested. To achieve victory, your total Power Level (Military + Structure) must be high enough to repel the enemy forces (at least 100). Fail, and your kingdom will be overrun.
